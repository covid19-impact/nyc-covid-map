NYC COVID-19 MORTALITY MAP

Hover, drag, and zoom to interact

Our interactive map visualizes the up-to-date total coronavirus deaths in each New York City zip code along with the population impact of the virus by zip code.

Beginning with data on May 18, 2020 when the city finally began to release zip code specific data, this visualization reveals the disparate impact of COVID-19 on NYC residents within a 302.64 sq mile area

The data sources include NYC Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) Data beginning on May 18, 2020
